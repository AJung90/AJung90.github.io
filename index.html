The Golden Age of Tennis

In today's game of tennis, we as an audience have been blessed with an opportunity to see three of the greatest players to ever step on the court. While there have been great tennis players in the past, never have we seen three players so dominant playing during the same era and fighting for the same trophies.

The three players, who undoubtedly would be the greatest in any other era are : Roger Federer, Rafael Nadal, and Novak Djokovic.

Here are a few lists of some of their most notable accomplishments, starting with Federer:

  • Has spent a record 302 weeks at #1
  • Owner of all 4 Grand Slam trophies
  • Has won a record 7 Wimbledon titles

Nadal:

  • One of only two players in history to have earned the "Golden Slam" (Winner of all 4 Grand Slams and Gold medal at the Olympics)
  • Has won a record 9 French Open titles

Djokovic

  • All time leader in career prize money (~$100M)
  • Has won a record 6 Australian Open titles
